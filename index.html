The MECH Empire

-=Welcome to the Machine=-

Sear the flesh, purify the soul, harden the heart, and improve the mind. Born of blood, but forged in fire, the MECH warrior reaches perfection.

The Empire of MECH is a Guild founded by Grand Master Rasta in the realm of Never Winter Nights. MECH is bent on cleansing the worlds of all lower forms of life (everything non-MECH). This is done through the skills, methods, and technology granted to us by Gond, the God of Metal.

MECH is composed of purified individuals who have had their past lives removed. Purification allows for said beings to begin a new life as MECH, provided they survive the process. Through this process they are stripped of their memories and reach a new level of existence after being altered, enhanced and molded into a superior form of life.

Many view our goals as evil, but upholding these principles forms the core of every MECH's programming, and is the only acceptable method of self preservation and advancement within the Empire. MECHs are mechanically efficient and cold, rarely displaying emotion of any kind.
